Coquito is a sweet alcoholic mix Puerto Ricans make during the holidays or for parties.

    Ingredients

  • rum
  • evaporated & condensed milk
  • cream of coconut
  • spices/cinnamon
  • egg yolks
  • vanilla extract
Coquito Recipe

    Steps

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. combine evaporated milk and egg yolks
  3. transfer mixture into a blender
  4. add everything else
  5. pour and chill with ice; or in the fridge, for 30 minutes
  6. after chilling, enjoy!
